Melinda Schneider

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia Melinda Schneider (born Melinda-Jane Bean; 7 October 1971) is an Australian country music singer.[1] She has been performing since she was three and sang with her mother, yodelling country artist Mary Schneider, on the album The Magic of Yodeling at the age of eight.[2] Schneider is a multi-Golden Guitar winner at the Country Music Awards of Australia winning her sixth with Paul Kelly for 'Vocal Collaboration of the Year' for their duet "Still Here" in 2009.[3]
